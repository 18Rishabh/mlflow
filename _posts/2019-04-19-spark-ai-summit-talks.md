[Spark + AI Summit 2019](https://databricks.com/sparkaisummit/north-america) runs next week from April 23rd to 25th in San Francisco, CA.
Approximately ten months after MLflow's launch last June, this Summit features eleven sessions on MLflow, including
presentations from Comcast, Showtime, R Studio, Splice Machine, GO-JEK, Databricks and Kount. You can find out more about
the upcoming talks in [this overview blog post from Databricks](https://databricks.com/blog/2019/04/18/a-guide-to-mlflow-talks-at-spark-ai-summit-2019.html).

The Spark + AI Summit keynotes will also be streamed on the [conference website](https://databricks.com/sparkaisummit/north-america).
